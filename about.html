About Us

Fresco was established in 2016 by Vikas Sharma and Thimira Bandara. We are a clothing company that stands for being fresh and loving the environment. We believe that it is our responsibility to help raise awareness and preserve the environment as much as we can. The dream is that we will be able to donate a portion of our sums to organizations such as Stand For Trees. With your help we can accomplish this goal. We hope to motivate the world to take action and explore more of the natural beauty in the world. Thank you for your support! And remember to be fresh, stay fresh, and live fresh!
